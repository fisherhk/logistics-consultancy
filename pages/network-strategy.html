Network Strategy & Design

Optimize your global logistics network for maximum efficiency

Strategic Network Planning for Global Success

Design and optimize your global logistics network with comprehensive strategic planning. Our Network Strategy & Design service helps you create efficient, cost-effective distribution networks that support your business objectives while maintaining flexibility for future growth.

What You'll Get

1

Network Assessment Report

Comprehensive analysis of your current logistics network, identifying strengths, weaknesses, and opportunities for optimization.

2

Governing Principles Framework

Detailed documentation of recommended legal structures, tax strategies, intra-company trading terms, and compliance requirements.

3

Optimized Network Design

Visual and analytical representation of the recommended network configuration, including facility locations, service areas, and flow patterns.

4

Financial Impact Analysis

Detailed cost-benefit analysis showing projected savings, implementation costs, and ROI for the recommended network design.

5

Implementation Roadmap

Step-by-step plan for transitioning from your current network to the optimized design, including timelines, milestones, and risk mitigation strategies.

Our Process

1

Discovery & Data Collection

We gather detailed information about your current network, volumes, costs, service requirements, and strategic objectives.

2

Network Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of your current state, identifying bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and opportunities for improvement.

3

Scenario Modeling

Development and evaluation of multiple network configurations using advanced optimization tools and industry best practices.

4

Recommendation Development

Creation of detailed recommendations including network design, governing principles, and implementation roadmap.

5

Presentation & Refinement

Review findings with your team, refine recommendations based on feedback, and finalize the strategic plan.

Information We Need

Current Network Data

  • Existing facility locations and capacities
  • Current distribution patterns and volumes
  • Inventory levels by location
  • Facility operating costs and lease terms

Demand & Customer Information

  • Customer locations and order patterns
  • Product mix and volume by region
  • Service level requirements
  • Seasonal demand fluctuations

Cost & Financial Data

  • Transportation rates by mode and lane
  • Warehousing and handling costs
  • Duty and tax rates by country
  • Inventory carrying costs
