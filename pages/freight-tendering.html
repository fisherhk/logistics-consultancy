Freight Tendering

Secure competitive rates through strategic procurement

Optimize Your Freight Procurement

Execute strategic freight tenders to secure competitive rates and favorable terms from ocean, air, and ground carriers. Our Freight Tendering service manages the entire RFP process—from data preparation and bid solicitation to analysis and carrier selection—ensuring you achieve optimal pricing while maintaining service quality and building strong carrier partnerships.

What You'll Get

1

Comprehensive RFP Package

Professional request for proposal documents with clear requirements, standardized formats, and detailed lane specifications.

2

Bid Analysis Report

Detailed comparison of all carrier proposals including cost modeling, service assessment, and risk evaluation.

3

Carrier Recommendation Matrix

Strategic recommendations for carrier selection by lane and mode with allocation strategies and fallback options.

4

Negotiated Rate Agreements

Finalized contracts with selected carriers including negotiated rates, terms, and service level commitments.

5

Implementation Plan

Transition roadmap for moving from incumbent carriers to new agreements with minimal service disruption.

Our Process

1

Data Preparation & Analysis

Collect and analyze historical shipment data, identify key lanes, and prepare detailed volume profiles for the RFP.

2

RFP Development & Distribution

Create comprehensive RFP documents and distribute to pre-qualified carriers with clear requirements and timelines.

3

Bid Collection & Clarification

Manage the bid submission process, conduct clarification calls, and ensure all proposals are complete and comparable.

4

Analysis & Negotiation

Analyze all bids using total cost models, conduct negotiations with selected carriers, and finalize terms.

5

Award & Implementation

Communicate award decisions, execute contracts, and support transition to new carrier arrangements.

Information We Need

Shipment Data

  • Historical shipment volumes by lane
  • Origin and destination locations
  • Commodity types and special requirements
  • Shipment weights and dimensions

Service Requirements

  • Transit time expectations by lane
  • Service level requirements (standard, expedited)
  • Equipment needs (dry van, reefer, flatbed)
  • Special handling or compliance needs

Current Rates & Performance

  • Current carrier contracts and rates
  • Accessorial charges and fuel surcharges
  • Carrier performance metrics
  • Total annual freight spend by mode
