Warehousing Tendering

Select the right warehousing partners through competitive bidding

Strategic Warehouse Partner Selection

Find and select optimal warehousing and fulfillment partners through structured competitive bidding. Our Warehousing Tendering service manages the complete 3PL selection process—from defining requirements and site evaluations to contract negotiation and implementation support—ensuring you secure cost-effective solutions with the capabilities to support your business needs.

What You'll Get

1

Requirements Definition Document

Detailed specification of warehousing requirements including space needs, services, technology, and performance expectations.

2

RFP Package & Responses

Professional request for proposal with standardized formats and organized responses from qualified 3PL providers.

3

Comparative Analysis Report

Comprehensive comparison of all proposals including cost modeling, capability assessment, and site evaluation summaries.

4

Provider Recommendation

Strategic recommendation for warehouse partner selection with supporting rationale and risk assessment.

5

Negotiated Service Agreement

Finalized contract with selected provider including rates, service levels, KPIs, and terms negotiated on your behalf.

Our Process

1

Requirements Definition

Work with your team to define warehouse requirements, service needs, volume profiles, and evaluation criteria.

2

Market Research & RFP

Identify qualified providers, develop comprehensive RFP documents, and manage the proposal submission process.

3

Proposal Analysis & Site Visits

Analyze all proposals, conduct site visits to shortlisted facilities, and assess operational capabilities.

4

Negotiation & Selection

Negotiate final terms with preferred providers and support your selection decision with detailed analysis.

5

Contract & Transition Support

Finalize contracts and provide implementation support to ensure smooth transition to the new provider.

Information We Need

Facility Requirements

  • Square footage needed (storage and processing)
  • Preferred geographic location and coverage area
  • Special facility needs (temperature control, hazmat)
  • Dock and trailer parking requirements

Operational Profile

  • Current and projected inventory volumes
  • Inbound and outbound transaction volumes
  • SKU count and product characteristics
  • Peak season requirements and fluctuations

Service & Technology Needs

  • Value-added services required (kitting, assembly)
  • WMS and system integration requirements
  • Reporting and visibility needs
  • Service level and KPI expectations
