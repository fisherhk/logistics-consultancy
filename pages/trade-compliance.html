Trade Compliance Advisory

Navigate global trade regulations with confidence

Expert Guidance for Complex Trade Regulations

Navigate the complexities of international trade compliance with expert guidance on customs regulations, trade agreements, product classification, and duty optimization. Our Trade Compliance Advisory service helps you minimize compliance risk while maximizing duty savings through strategic use of Free Trade Agreements and proper classification strategies.

What You'll Get

1

Compliance Assessment Report

Comprehensive review of your current trade compliance practices, identifying gaps, risks, and opportunities for improvement.

2

HS Code Classification Guide

Product-specific classification recommendations with supporting rationale and alternative classifications where applicable.

3

FTA Qualification Analysis

Detailed analysis of Free Trade Agreement eligibility for your products and recommendations for maximizing duty savings.

4

Compliance Procedures Manual

Documented procedures for import/export operations, recordkeeping requirements, and compliance management protocols.

5

Implementation Roadmap

Prioritized action plan for implementing compliance improvements and realizing duty savings opportunities.

Our Process

1

Compliance Review

Assess current import/export processes, documentation practices, and compliance controls to identify risks and gaps.

2

Product Classification

Review and optimize HS code classifications for your product portfolio with supporting technical analysis and rationale.

3

FTA Qualification

Analyze eligibility for Free Trade Agreement benefits and develop strategies for origin qualification and documentation.

4

Procedure Development

Create or enhance compliance procedures, internal controls, and documentation management systems.

5

Training & Implementation

Train your team on compliance requirements and support implementation of recommended improvements.

Information We Need

Product Information

  • Detailed product descriptions and specifications
  • Current HS code classifications
  • Materials composition and technical documentation
  • Country of origin and manufacturing processes

Trade Flow Data

  • Import and export volumes by country
  • Trading partners and entity relationships
  • Current duty payments and tax liabilities
  • FTA programs currently utilized

Compliance Documentation

  • Existing customs procedures and policies
  • Previous audit reports or findings
  • Current recordkeeping practices
  • Broker and import/export licenses
