Benchmarking Services

Compare your logistics performance against industry standards and best practices

Measure Performance, Drive Improvement

Understand how your logistics operations compare to industry peers and best-in-class performers with our Benchmarking Services. We provide data-driven insights into your freight costs, service provider rates, operational performance, and process maturity—helping you identify improvement opportunities and validate that you're getting competitive value from your logistics investments.

What You'll Get

1

Rate Benchmarking Analysis

Comparison of your freight and warehousing rates versus market averages by lane, mode, and service type.

2

Operational Performance Report

Assessment of key performance indicators including on-time delivery, order accuracy, claims rates, and cycle times.

3

Process Maturity Assessment

Evaluation of your logistics processes, technology utilization, and organizational capabilities against industry best practices.

4

Gap Analysis & Recommendations

Detailed analysis of performance gaps with prioritized recommendations for improvement initiatives.

5

Executive Summary Dashboard

Visual presentation of key findings with easy-to-understand comparisons and actionable insights for leadership.

Our Process

1

Scope Definition

Define benchmarking scope including metrics, geographies, business units, and peer group selection criteria.

2

Data Collection

Gather your internal data on costs, rates, volumes, and performance metrics across all relevant logistics activities.

3

Comparative Analysis

Compare your metrics against our proprietary benchmark database and conduct peer group analysis.

4

Gap Identification

Identify performance gaps, root causes, and specific opportunities for improvement across all benchmarked areas.

5

Recommendations & Roadmap

Develop prioritized recommendations with implementation roadmap to close gaps and improve performance.

Information We Need

Cost & Rate Data

  • Freight rates by mode, lane, and carrier
  • Warehousing costs (storage, handling, labor)
  • Total logistics spend by category
  • Cost per unit or as percentage of revenue

Operational Metrics

  • On-time delivery performance
  • Order accuracy and fulfillment rates
  • Inventory turns and carrying costs
  • Claims rates and damage statistics

Process & Technology

  • Systems used (TMS, WMS, ERP)
  • Level of automation and integration
  • Process documentation and SOPs
  • Team size and organizational structure
