Transactional Freight Management

Expert execution of your day-to-day freight operations

Operational Excellence, Every Shipment

Let our team handle the day-to-day execution of your freight operations with Transactional Freight Management services. We manage carrier booking, documentation, customs clearance, and shipment tracking across all modes—leveraging our expertise, carrier relationships, and global network to ensure reliable service while you focus on your core business.

What You'll Get

1

Dedicated Service Team

Assigned team of logistics professionals who learn your business and provide personalized service.

2

End-to-End Shipment Management

Complete handling of bookings, documentation, carrier coordination, tracking, and delivery confirmation.

3

Real-Time Visibility Portal

Access to online portal with shipment tracking, documentation, reporting, and analytics capabilities.

4

Performance Reports & Analytics

Regular reporting on shipment performance, costs, service levels, and carrier performance metrics.

5

Proactive Issue Resolution

24/7 monitoring and proactive intervention to resolve issues before they impact your business.

Our Process

1

Order Receipt & Validation

Receive shipment requests through your preferred method (EDI, email, portal) and validate all details.

2

Carrier Selection & Booking

Select optimal carrier based on your routing guide, secure space, and confirm booking details.

3

Documentation & Compliance

Prepare all shipping documentation, arrange customs clearance, and ensure regulatory compliance.

4

Tracking & Monitoring

Monitor shipment progress in real-time, provide proactive updates, and address any exceptions immediately.

5

Delivery & Reporting

Confirm delivery, resolve any delivery issues, and provide documentation and performance reporting.

Information We Need

Shipment Profile

  • Expected shipment volumes by mode and lane
  • Typical shipment sizes and frequencies
  • Product types and special handling needs
  • Peak periods and seasonality patterns

Service Requirements

  • Service level expectations and KPIs
  • Preferred carriers and routing guides
  • Documentation and compliance requirements
  • Communication and reporting preferences

Systems & Integration

  • Current TMS or order management system
  • EDI or API integration capabilities
  • Data format and transmission protocols
  • Visibility and reporting requirements
